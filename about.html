Tau Epsilon Kappa is a co-ed professional technical fraternity committed to professional technical development as well as the advancement of social and academic excellence among the fraternity brothers. We strive to provide opportunities for our members to explore their technical interests and further their relationships in both a social and professional environment. Tau Epsilon Kappa fosters members who are passionate to cultivate their skills to help better the community, as well as encourage a diverse, inclusive, and equal community.

Come meet your fellow tech enthusiasts!

What We Stand For

Integrity

Our fraternity is comprised of students with who are truly committed to upholding their morals and values. At Tau Epsilon Kappa, we believe that honestry and accountability are incredibly important. We aim to reflect well not only on our own characters, but on the character of our fraternity as a whole.

Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion

At Tau Epsilon Kappa, we know that a diverse, equitable, and inclusive environment is a more innovative and successful one. Within our fraternity, we embrace diversity of backgrounds, perspectives, cultures, and experiences.The values are integral to our mission, and we hope to change the social and cultural aspects of the technology industry for the better.

Excellence

At Tau Epsilon Kappa, we strive for excellence in all aspects of life. Not only are our brothers hard-working, dedicated students, but they are also leaders that choose to make a difference in areas that they care about and role models that lead others to follow in their paths. We truly value commitment from all our brothers to be their best selves in all aspects.
